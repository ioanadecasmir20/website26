How to apply

To be considered for current or future roles with LS Security, please email your CV and a clear copy of your SIA licence to info@lssecurity.co.uk with the role you are interested in.

  • Valid SIA licence (Door Supervisor, Security Guarding or CCTV)
  • Right to work in the UK and a 5-year checkable history
  • Excellent communication and customer service skills
  • Professional appearance and timekeeping

We review applications on an ongoing basis and will contact suitable candidates when shifts become available.

Tell us about your site, event or organisation and we’ll come back with a clear proposal, including pricing, deployment options and recommended officer numbers.
